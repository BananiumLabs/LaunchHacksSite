FAQ

Who can attend?

LaunchHacks is open to all high school students (9th to 12th grade). However, if you have already graduated from high school, you can still attend as a mentor!

What is the cost for admission?

Attending LaunchHacks is and will always be free!

What can my team do?

Anything within reason! Whatever awesome project you decide to make is fair game! Software, hardware, crazy inventions, or spooky voodoo magic are all allowed, but please remember that you will be presenting your project to expert judges who will decide if you project is worth an award. More information about our judging criteria is avaliable below!

If you don't know what to make, feel free to come to any of our workshops or ask an organizer! We are here to help!

How many people can be in a team?

Teams can be a maximum of 4 people. Solo teams are allowed, but we strongly encourage you to meet new people if you come by yourself! We will do our best to help everyone find a team.

Don't know anyone? Take initiative and reach out to those who RSVP'd!

Am I required to stay overnight?

If you need to leave early for any reason, please let us know through email before the day of the event. For safety reasons we will not allow anyone to leave after 8 PM. Doors will open at 9 AM in time for judging.

Do I have to know how to code?

No. However, it might be a good opportunity to learn and get started! Please refer to the judging parameter for more information.

How will our project be judged?

All projects will be judged with the same criteria: originality, polish, difficulty of implementation, and viability as a product. We highly suggest that you treat your project like a product that you could potentially sell- rather than pitching just for the judges, pitch for everyone else, too!
